About Me

Andrea Pünchera

My name is Andrea Pünchera, I grew up in Switzerland and used to ski race professionally until the age of 22.

After that, I studied Finance and Banking and started my career with EY in Transaction Advisory working on M&A deals doing Financial Due Diligence and Valuation and Business Modelling Projects. I then moved on to Strategy within EY-Parthenon and for the past 3.5 years helped build a Swiss Fintech in Zurich.

I'm Passionate about Entrepreneurship, Technology, Sports, Social Impact, and Venture Capital.
